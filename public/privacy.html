Privacy Policy

At Edutho, we respect and protect your privacy. Edutho is a secure, no-login online notepad for creating and sharing private notes or code snippets. This policy explains how we safeguard your information.

1. No Account & No Personal Identity Required

You do not need to provide your name, email, or any personal identifying data to use Edutho. We do not create user profiles or track individual identity.

2. Note Storage & Handling

- Notes are stored anonymously using a random or custom URL.
- If you set a password, it is securely hashed — we cannot read it.
- Notes may be automatically deleted after long-term inactivity or if empty.

3. Analytics & Access Logging

We use analytics tools to understand general usage patterns (such as device type and approximate region). We do not use analytics to personally identify any user.

4. Cookies

Edutho does not use marketing or behavioral tracking cookies. Some third-party widgets (such as donation tools) may set necessary functional cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

We may integrate third-party services such as Google Analytics or Buy Me a Coffee. These services operate independently and have their own privacy policies. We never sell or share note content or identifying data with any third party.

6. Data Security

We apply protections including encryption of stored content, hashed passwords, restricted database access, and scheduled cleanup of expired notes.

7. International Privacy Standards

Edutho is designed to align with global data protection standards such as GDPR (EU) and PIPEDA (Canada) by minimizing data collection and ensuring anonymous usage.

8. Children’s Privacy

Edutho is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

9. Your Right to Request Deletion

If you created a note and wish to permanently delete it, you may email us with the note link and we will remove it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy over time. Any changes will be published on this page.
